## Overview

Cancelling meetings with AI Voice Agents involves handling inbound calls and using connected calendars to remove existing events.

## Steps to Cancel a Meeting

1. **Call Reception**:
   - When a call is received on the assigned phone number, the agent will answer the call.

2. **Request Cancellation**:
   - Inform the agent that you want to cancel an existing meeting.
   - Provide the necessary details of the meeting to be canceled (e.g., date, time etc.).

3. **Confirm Cancellation**:
   - The agent will confirm the details of the meeting to ensure accuracy.

4. **Update Calendar**:
   - The agent will remove the meeting from your connected calendar.

This process ensures efficient cancellation of meetings through AI Voice Agents using inbound calls and integrated calendar services.
